ONBOARD

No hassle of carrying luggage to the airport, check it in from home!


OVERVIEW

As a travel enthusiast, I've been to a lot of places and taken a ton of flights. But no matter how many times I fly, I always feel a little stressed on travel day, even if I'm super prepared. This is what inspired me to create a product that can help to reduce the stress experienced by the travelers on the day of their flight.

TIME DURATION

3 Months

MY ROLE

UI/UX Designer

TOOLS USED

PROBLEM STATEMENT v1

How might we make air travel less stressful?

SECONDARY RESEARCH

After reading online articles about the various factors that contribute to stress before air travel, I discovered that...

there is no single problem that causes stress, but different individual experiences stress at different stages of the air travel process.

USER SURVEY

I conducted a survey on 34 Indian participants to better understand and narrow down the issues faced by Indian travelers, as the online articles I had previously referenced in my secondary research were from different countries and did not necessarily reflect the situation in India.

Insights:

Stressed users

  • Married Millenials

  • Senior Citizens

Travel Partners

  • Female Travelers: Solo

  • Married Millenials: Kids

  • Senior Citizens: Family

Convenience

  • Most of the users prefer private taxi over public transport.

Most stressful part

  • Reaching to the airport

  • Passing Security

  • Baggage collection

USER INTERVIEW

Based on the findings from user survey, I interviewed 4 users (2 young female, a user between age 30-40 and a senior citizen). Below are few of the questions which I asked them during the interview:


  • Why reaching to the airport is a stressful task according to you?

  • Why do you choose private taxi over public transport? (Where do you think public transport lacks as compared to private taxis?)

  • How far is airport in your city from the place where you live and how much time does it take you to reach the airport? How much does it cost to take the private taxi? Do you feel relaxed after boarding the taxi on time from your home? If not, then why?

  • How air travel with family is different than traveling solo?

  • Why security check and luggage check-in is a stressful task according to you?

  • Did you ever face any major problem in your past travels during security check or luggage check-in at the airport?

Insights:

Stressing factor

  • Fear of reaching late to the airport

  • Managing kids/babies and luggage together at airport.

  • Long queues at security & luggage check-in.

Luggage

  • Difficult to handle luggage in public transport.

  • Extra luggage gets added when traveling with babies/kids

  • Seniors often travel with family, that’s why it is not an the issue.

Convenience

  • Taxis are easily available and convenient when traveling with lots of luggage and family members, that’s why users prefer it over public transport.

Time & Distance

  • Airports are far away from users home

  • Even after boarding the taxi, users continue to experience stress until they arrive at the airport on time.

SOLUTION

A Progressive Web App(PWA) for the pick up and check-in of luggage from the comfort of the user's home,

eliminating the stress and inconvenience of carrying luggage to the airport and avoiding long check-in lines


COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Companies cancelling booking just before pickup time

Sudden cancellation cause panic and stress for users as they are then forced to carry their luggage to the airport.

Mishandling of the luggage either by the airline or by the pickup company

Sudden cancellation cause panic and stress for users as they are then forced to carry their luggage to the airport.

Opportunity #1

HMW make sure that no bookings get cancelled?


With adequate resources and routing software, the sole issue for the company is their inability to execute user pickups and ensure timely check-in at the airport. This arises from the time constraint, where there is insufficient time for the driver to travel to the user's location, collect the luggage, bring it to the airport, and complete check-in.


Solution:

Offering additional discount on a specific time slot for a specific pickup area.

This will encourage users of a specific area to choose pickup in specific time slot which will simplify the pickup process for the driver, allowing them to complete pickups in one area before moving to another. This eliminates the need for constant back and forth travel between areas, saving time which means completing all the scheduled bookings and no cancellations!

Opportunity #2

HMW make sure that no luggage is mishandled?


Where is it going wrong?

If the company is solely held accountable for luggage mishandling, then where is it going wrong?


Incorrect labelling

Incorrect labeling of luggage by the pickup agent causing bags to be loaded onto incorrect flights and ending up at unintended destinations.

Lack of infrastructure

For example, in a warehouse with 200 bags, it can be challenging for an individual to determine which bags need to be checked in and at...

For example, in a warehouse with 200 bags, it can be challenging for an individual to determine which bags need to be checked in and at what time, as the flights associated with each bag may have different departure times (e.g. 11:00 am or 5:00 pm). Automation is necessary to manage this task effectively.

Incorrect boarding

While it is unlikely, there is a possibility of an airport agent checking in luggage under the wrong airline. However, the luggage is connected...

While it is unlikely, there is a possibility of an airport agent checking in luggage under the wrong airline. However, the luggage is connected to a specific airline ticket, which is checked by the airline staff during check-in, reducing the chance of such an error occurring.


Solution:

Use QR-code enabled Onboard bag tags.


  • Ask users to verify their personal and flight details on Onboard bag tags at the time of pickup.

  • Implement the use of QR codes to scan luggage at warehouse, allowing the system to access flight information so that it can alert the staff to check in the bags on time.

  • Upload pictures of Airline bag tag and Onboard tag with all the details clearly visible, ensuring their peace of mind that their bags have been checked in to the correct flight.

LOFI SKETCHES

Things I kept in mind before drawing sketches

  • As the concept of checking in luggage from home is relatively new in India and not widely known, so try to explain what company does, their operational procedures, the geographical coverage of our service, the benefits it offers to customers, and establish trust and reliability among users.

  • During the booking process, allow users to check if the service is available in their area and on their flight or not before anything else.

  • Keep the booking steps similar to the standard flight booking process, as this is a familiar and user-friendly format for most users.

USABILITY TEST

I conducted a moderated usability test to four participants who belong to our user personas. During the test, I asked them to complete below tasks:

TASKS

  • Check the price and book the pickup service for 2 bags of you and your friend for the flight from Delhi to Leh.

  • Make modifications to the existing luggage pickup reservation for the Delhi to Leh flight, where you need to re-schedule the flight to the one which departs at 10:00 am.

  • Check the final status of your luggage to find out if it's successfully checked in or not?
