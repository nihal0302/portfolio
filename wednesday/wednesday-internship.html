OVERVIEW

During my internship, I designed and developed a fully responsive website with integrated CMS and e-commerce capabilities from scratch. The internship began with comprehensive research of the Webflow marketplace to identify popular website topics and existing templates. Later, I conducted user and template buyer research, created mood boards, developed style guides, and produced wireframes and high-fidelity designs. After completing the designs, I started with the development phase using Webflow. Throughout development, I adhered to specific guidelines to maintain quality, consistency and technical compatibilty. It took me three months to design, develop, and get approval from the Webflow team to successfully list the website on the marketplace.
